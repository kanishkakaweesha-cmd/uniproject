About ETRACKER

ETRACKER is an advanced web application that integrates with ESP32 to capture real-time data from connected devices. It simplifies the process of monitoring and managing delivery operations.

With automatic label generation and weight-based item tracking, ETRACKER ensures accuracy and efficiency, making packaging and delivery smarter than ever.
